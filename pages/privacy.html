Privacy Policy

This policy outlines the principles and guidelines governing access to and usage of the online library resources provided by Silent Library. It aims to ensure equitable access, responsible use, and the preservation of intellectual property.
This policy applies to all users of the online library, including but not limited to: registered members, staff, and authorized guests.

Principles:
Open Access (Where Applicable): We are committed to providing access to a wide range of resources, including open-access materials, to support learning, research, and personal enrichment.

Copyright Compliance: Users must adhere to all applicable copyright laws and licensing agreements. Unauthorized reproduction, distribution, or modification of copyrighted materials is strictly prohibited.

Responsible Use: Users are expected to use the online library resources responsibly and ethically, respecting the rights of others and the integrity of the information provided.

Privacy and Security: We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring the security of personal information. Data collection and usage will be conducted in accordance with our privacy policy.

Accessibility: We strive to make our online library resources accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, in compliance with accessibility standards.

Resource Preservation: We are committed to preserving the integrity and long-term accessibility of our digital collections.

Guidelines:
Account Access: Access to certain online library resources may require a valid user account. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

Permitted Use:
Permitted uses of online library resources may include:
• Reading and viewing materials for personal or educational purposes.

• Downloading and printing materials for personal or educational purposes, within the limits of copyright and licensing agreements.

• Citing and referencing materials in accordance with academic standards.

Prohibited Use:
Prohibited uses of online library resources include:
• Unauthorized copying, distribution, or modification of copyrighted materials.

• Commercial use of online library resources without explicit permission.

• Attempting to circumvent security measures or gain unauthorized access to the system.

• Engaging in any activity that disrupts or interferes with the operation of the online library.

• Systematic downloading or archiving of entire databases or e-book collections.

Resource Availability: The availability of specific online library resources may vary depending on licensing agreements and technical limitations.

Reporting Violations: Users are encouraged to report any suspected violations of this policy to Silent Library.

Policy Review: This policy will be reviewed and updated periodically to ensure its continued relevance and effectiveness.

Enforcement:
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or termination of library privileges.

Disclaimer:
Silent Library is not responsible for the accuracy or completeness of all information provided through the online library.

Contact Information:
For questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us via the contact methods here.

For our digital services:

a. We may use "cookies", where a small data file is sent to your browser to store and track information about you when you enter our digital services. The cookie is used to track information such as the number of users and their frequency of use, profiles of users and their preferred digital services. While this cookie can tell us when you enter our digital services and which pages you visit, it cannot read data off your hard disk.

b. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the digital services.

If you provide us with personal data:

a. We may use the data (such as to process, store and retain it) so as to improve the performance of our functions and to serve you in the most efficient and effective way. This includes providing updates and recommendations on Silent Library content and services or personalising services for you.

b. Where appropriate, we may share necessary data with other Agencies, so as to improve the wider performance of public functions and to serve you in the most efficient and effective way unless such sharing is prohibited by law.

To safeguard your personal data, all electronic storage and transmission of personal data is secured with appropriate security technologies.
